STEMify Project

About the Project

STEMify is a career guidance platform for students fresh from high school. It helps them explore career paths in STEM fields, learn about first-year modules, and discover further study opportunities.

Project Objectives

  • Guide students in selecting STEM careers
  • Match career options with educational pathways
  • Provide access to mentors and resources
  • Help students set academic and career goals
